Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get approved for a loan?

Our loan approval process typically takes 3-15 min once all required documents are submitted. For urgent applications, we offer expedited processing within 2-24 hours during business hours.

What documents do I need to apply for a loan?

You'll need:

  • Valid government-issued ID (National ID or Passport)
  • Proof of income (salary slips, bank statements)
  • Employment verification letter
  • Bank statements for the last 3 months
  • Proof of residence (utility bill or rental agreement)
What are your interest rates and loan terms?

Our interest rates start from 12% per annum and vary based on your credit profile and loan amount. Loan terms range from 6 months to 60 months. The exact rate and terms will be provided during the application process based on your eligibility assessment.

How can I make loan payments?

We offer multiple payment options:

  • Online payments through your account dashboard
  • Mobile money (M-Pesa, Airtel Money)
  • Bank transfers
  • Direct debit from your bank account
  • Cash payments at partner locations
What happens if I miss a payment?

If you miss a payment, we'll contact you within 24 hours to discuss payment options. Late fees may apply after 7 days. We offer flexible payment arrangements for customers experiencing temporary financial difficulties. It's important to contact us as soon as possible if you anticipate payment issues.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, absolutely. We use bank-level 256-bit SSL encryption to protect all data transmissions. Your personal and financial information is stored on secure servers and is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent. We comply with all Kenyan data protection regulations.
