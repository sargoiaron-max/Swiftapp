5. Repayment Rules & Procedures

Standard Repayment Terms

  • Repayment period: 30 days from loan disbursement
  • Monthly interest rate: 0.86% of principal amount
  • Grace period: 3 days after due date without penalty
  • Accepted payment methods: M-Pesa, bank transfer
  • Minimum partial payment: 25% of total amount due

Payment Reminders

Reminder Schedule

  • ‚Ä¢ 7 days before due date - First reminder SMS
  • ‚Ä¢ 3 days before due date - Email and SMS reminder
  • ‚Ä¢ Due date - Final reminder notification
  • ‚Ä¢ 1 day after due date - Overdue notice
  • ‚Ä¢ 7 days overdue - Collection process begins

Early Repayment Benefits

  • No penalties for early repayment
  • Interest calculated only for days loan was outstanding
  • Faster eligibility for higher loan amounts
  • Improved credit score and future terms
