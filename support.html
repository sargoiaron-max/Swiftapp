Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can I get a loan?

Most loans are approved and disbursed within 2-5 minutes during business hours. The process is completely automated for eligible customers.

What are the interest rates?

Our monthly interest rate is 0.86% of the principal amount. There are no hidden fees - all charges are clearly displayed before you accept the loan.

Can I repay early without penalties?

Yes! You can repay your loan early without any penalties. Interest is calculated only for the days you actually used the loan.

What happens if I miss a payment?

You have a 3-day grace period without penalties. After that, a 1% daily penalty applies. Contact us immediately if you're facing payment difficulties.

How can I increase my loan limit?

Make on-time payments to build your credit history with us. Each successful repayment increases your eligibility for higher loan amounts.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we use bank-level security with end-to-end encryption. We comply with Kenya's Data Protection Act and never share your data without consent.
